Ethiopia

Change in Power
#75YT5T

In Ethiopia, 50% of the population lives under the poverty line and basic internet is also not available there. But, a recent Govt. has got power and the certain changes observed are - Lesser Corruption, Equality on rise, 50% of the cabinet ministry are women and the president herself. And the Economy is increasing by 10% for 100,000,000 people every year. And all these happened within just 6 months. *(Data fetched maybe subject to change as the given data is as per as 2019 - Before Pandemic-)
