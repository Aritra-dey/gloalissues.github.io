Sierre Leone

Emmanuel's Solar Car
#789YF2

In Freetown, Sierre Leone, Emmanuel, a young man built a solar car from trash. Pollution was a major problem for the people of the town and Emmanuel tried to solve that problem. He has more than 50 solutions to environmental problems. While initiating his work, he didn’t have the money to be paid but he, with his intelligence, collected scraps and trash from junkyards and landfills. He tried and his idea evolved and he took 3 years to complete the car. The car has solar panels on it’s roof and runs on Solar Power and can accelerate to 15km/hr. A YouTube channel, NasDaily, revealed his creation to the whole world.
