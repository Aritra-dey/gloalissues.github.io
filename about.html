FREEDOM OF SPEECH & EXPRESSION

This discusses all the points, this platform covers under the act of Liberty of Speech and Expression and must be abided while sharing any sharable thing. One must abide by these points:-

You may disagree with a policy of the government or activities of an association and you’re free to criticize them. (Read Our Govt. Criticizing Policies). One may publicise his/her perspective or views through a pamphlet, magazine, newspaper or a digital media, etc.

While exercising liberty of speech and expression, you cannot use this freedom as a tool to instigate violence against others. You cannot use it to incite people to rebel against the exercising government. Neither can you use it to defame others by false means and mean things that cause damage to a person’s reputation.

You must not influence the General Public to stop supporting a development or emergency campaign runned by the Govt. You can promote but can’t abuse. Criticizing the Govt. reflects that you’re a responsible citizen and awaring the government about it’s actions and policies but misuse or abuse of this power is highly discouraged.
